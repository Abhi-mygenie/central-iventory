## What was done

All 4 phases + GST fix implemented in a single session:

1. **Phase 1:** `give_discount` mapped through pipeline (`productTransform` → `adaptProduct` → `fromAPI.orderItem`). Boolean `giveDiscount` on every cart item.

2. **Phase 2:** `discountableTotal` computed in both `CartPanel` and `CollectPaymentPanel`. % discounts use discountable base. Flat ₹ capped at `discountableTotal`.

3. **Phase 3:** `distributeItemDiscounts()` helper (largest-remainder proportional). Injected into Flow 3 (`placeOrderWithPayment`) and Flow 4 (`collectBillExisting`). Flows 1/2 untouched (no discounts).

4. **Phase 3B:** Coupon item/category targeting via CRM `benefit_items`. BOGO = `discount_amount` = `food_amount`. E-5 guard rejects coupon if target has `giveDiscount=false`. `benefitItems` threaded through discounts object.

5. **GST Fix:** Per-item `gst_amount` and `vat_amount` recomputed on `(fullLine - discount_amount)` in Flows 3 & 4 post-processing. Verified: Σ per-item GST = order-level `gst_tax`.

## Key design decisions

- `discount_amount` at item level = ₹ of discount (not post-discount amount). `'0.00'` = no discount.
- `discount_value` at order level = unchanged. NOT sent at item level.
- `gst_amount` / `vat_amount` at item level = post-discount tax (recomputed from source item's `.tax` info).
- `buildCartItem()` still emits pre-discount values — overridden by post-processing in Flows 3/4 only.
- `calcOrderTotals` and panel display GST unchanged — uniform `discountRatio` gives correct order-level totals.
- Coupon and manual/preset mutually exclusive (code-verified).
- QSR CartPanel has no coupon support — `couponDiscount: 0` hardcoded. Phase 3B only touches CollectPaymentPanel.
